Calendar Importer

This is a simple Google Calendar sharing and importing service. By granting this website access to your calendars a list of your Google Calendars will be shown with the possibility to copy a shareable link and send it to friends.

With the shared link your friends can subscribe to your calendar in various ways. The only prerequisite for it to work is that the calendar is already publicly accessible.

Note: This is not an official Google product. The term "Google Calendar" is solely used to reference to Google's product and the calendars provided by that.
